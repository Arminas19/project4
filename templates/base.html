Something About Us

Sodja Resturaunt

We promise a romantic setting for you and your guessed to dine in. We offer a special menu that was selected by our head chef that should complement your romantic environment. We have Comtrouble chairs with nicely dressed tables and a bar in our restaurant. We also have a decorated lounge area if for any reason you had to wait for your tables to get pre-pared. Also something interesting about our bar - our bar got nominated for Best restaurant Bar of the Year 2022. At Arkle Bar, each and every dish is freshly prepared and cooked and is a dedication to our promise to use only the best quality ingredients. Dine in our exquisite, stylish Arkle Bar boasting its own unique style and ambiance, the perfect choice for food lovers and wine connoisseurs alike. Sink back into our plush 1920s interiors where you may choose to sip on a revitalising cocktail created by our in-house mixologist, savour a fine wine, whiskey or brandy.
